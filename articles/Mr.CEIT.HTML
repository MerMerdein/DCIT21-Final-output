Mike Casaverde Crowned Mr. CEIT

In Spectacular Pageant Finale

In a dazzling display of talent, charisma, and style, Mike Casaverde from Mr. ELITS has been crowned Mr. CEIT, taking home the coveted title in what proved to be a fiercely competitive pageant organized by CEIT Student Council.

The grand event, themed "Rise of Royalty," showcased the best and brightest male students from different organizations within CEIT, each representing their respective groups with pride and distinction.

The Winning Moment

Casaverde's victory was marked by an impressive performance throughout the competition. Wearing an elegant black suit adorned with blue floral embellishments and a striking white feathered accessory, he commanded the stage with confidence and poise. His winning combination of stage presence, personality, and overall performance set him apart from the other talented contestants.

First Runner-Up: Juri Chuks of Mr. UAPSA

Securing the first runner-up position was Juri Chuks, representing Mr. UAPSA. Chuks made a memorable impression in his sophisticated all-black ensemble featuring intricate beaded detailing. His dynamic stage presence and charismatic energy throughout the competition earned him strong support from the audience and judges alike, making him a worthy contender for the crown.

Second Runner-Up: Lance Sangangbayan of Mr. PICE

Rounding out the top three was Lance Sangangbayan from Mr. PICE, who claimed the second runner-up spot. Sangangbayan showcased his versatility and style in a stunning royal blue Filipino-inspired outfit with silver embroidery, paired with a formal white shirt and decorative bow tie. His sophisticated presentation and cultural pride resonated throughout his performance.
