Cavite State University - Main Campus

Published: January 22, 1998 | By: American Thomasites

The mandate of CvSU is "to provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology through quality instruction, and responsive research and development activities. It shall produce professional, skilled and morally upright individuals for global competitiveness."
